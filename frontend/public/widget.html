HR Chatbot — Embeddable Widget

This page demonstrates the embeddable chat widget. In production, add this snippet to any webpage:

<script src="https://your-domain.com/widget.js"
        data-api-url="https://your-domain.com"
        data-api-key="YOUR_API_KEY"></script>

The widget appears in the bottom-right corner. Click the green button to open it.
